# Evidence Classification Guide

## SEC_PUBLIC_FILING
- Description: Public SEC filing records.
- Examples: 10-K, 10-Q, 8-K, DEF 14A.
- Verification requirement: Validate accession and source URL.
- Risk of overstatement: Low if cited accurately.
- Recommended claim-verification use: Baseline documentary record.

## ESG_PUBLIC_STATEMENT
- Description: Public ESG reports and sustainability statements.
- Examples: Annual ESG report, climate commitments.
- Verification requirement: Confirm publication source and date.
- Risk of overstatement: Medium (marketing framing risk).
- Recommended claim-verification use: Compare narrative claims to records.

## MARKETING_PUBLIC_CLAIM
- Description: Public marketing or investor-facing operational claims.
- Examples: Product pages, press claims, investor decks.
- Verification requirement: Archive page snapshot and timestamp.
- Risk of overstatement: Medium to high.
- Recommended claim-verification use: Candidate claims for contradiction testing.

## FIRSTHAND_TESTIMONY
- Description: Statements from direct witnesses.
- Examples: Interview notes, declarations.
- Verification requirement: Identity, context, and corroboration checks.
- Risk of overstatement: Medium without corroboration.
- Recommended claim-verification use: Supporting context with caution labels.

## PHOTO_VIDEO_EVIDENCE
- Description: Image/video source material.
- Examples: Equipment photos, incident video.
- Verification requirement: Metadata validation and chain-of-custody.
- Risk of overstatement: Medium (context can be incomplete).
- Recommended claim-verification use: Corroborative support.

## MACHINE_LOG
- Description: Machine-generated telemetry and diagnostics.
- Examples: PLC logs, HMI event logs.
- Verification requirement: Source system provenance and timestamp integrity.
- Risk of overstatement: Medium if parsing is incomplete.
- Recommended claim-verification use: High-value contradiction/support evidence.

## VENDOR_RECORD
- Description: Third-party vendor maintenance or service records.
- Examples: Service tickets, repair invoices.
- Verification requirement: Vendor authenticity and scope matching.
- Risk of overstatement: Medium.
- Recommended claim-verification use: Corroborate operational claims.

## MAINTENANCE_RECORD
- Description: Internal maintenance logs.
- Examples: Work orders, downtime sheets.
- Verification requirement: Custodian confirmation and date continuity.
- Risk of overstatement: Medium.
- Recommended claim-verification use: Operational timeline checks.

## ENVIRONMENTAL_RECORD
- Description: Environmental handling and classification records.
- Examples: Waste classification forms, compliance logs.
- Verification requirement: Jurisdiction and facility mapping.
- Risk of overstatement: Medium to high.
- Recommended claim-verification use: Regulatory consistency testing.

## GENERATED_ANALYSIS
- Description: Locally generated analytical outputs.
- Examples: Forensic summaries, manifests, scorecards.
- Verification requirement: Traceability to source evidence.
- Risk of overstatement: High if detached from source citations.
- Recommended claim-verification use: Internal synthesis only.

## UNVERIFIED_LEAD
- Description: Preliminary leads awaiting validation.
- Examples: Tips, uncorroborated notes.
- Verification requirement: Independent corroboration required.
- Risk of overstatement: High.
- Recommended claim-verification use: Investigation queue only.
