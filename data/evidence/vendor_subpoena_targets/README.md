# Vendor Subpoena Targets

This directory holds documentation related to third-party records requests and subpoena target analysis.

## Purpose

Identifies categories of records held by third-party vendors and internal departments that may be relevant to an investigative inquiry. The records categories listed here reflect systems and processes that, if subpoenaed or voluntarily produced, could corroborate or contradict specific claims under review.

## Contents

- `subpoena_target_index.md` — categorized index of records request targets

## Important Note

Inclusion in this directory does not imply that any vendor or individual has acted improperly. These are records categories whose production would permit independent verification of claims under review.

All descriptions should use language consistent with the investigative language guide: `docs/reporting/INVESTIGATIVE_LANGUAGE_GUIDE.md`.
