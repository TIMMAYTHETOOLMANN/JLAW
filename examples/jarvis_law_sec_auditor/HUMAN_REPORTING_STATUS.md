## 📈 **VISUAL ANALYTICS EXPLAINED**

### **1. Transaction Timeline**

**Purpose**: **"Because there's no way it's sporadic"** - Your exact words

**What It Shows**:
- Every transaction plotted by date
- Bubble size = share volume
- Color = transaction code (D=red, A=green, etc.)
- **RED ZONES** = Clusters (3+ transactions within 5 days)

**Interpretation**:
- Sporadic = Normal insider activity
- Clustered = **Systematic fraud pattern**
- This graph **exposes coordinated manipulation**

### **2. Filing Delay Heat Map**

**Purpose**: Identify late filing patterns

**What It Shows**:
- Monthly average delay (bar chart)
- Color zones: Green (on-time), Yellow (warning), Red (critical)
- Threshold lines at 15 and 30 days

**Interpretation**:
- Consistent green = Compliant
- Increasing yellow/red = **Deteriorating controls**
- All red = **Systematic non-compliance**

### **3. Risk Bubble Chart**

**Purpose**: Show risk concentration

**What It Shows**:
- X-axis = Date
- Y-axis = Risk score (0-1)
- Bubble size = Transaction volume
- Color intensity = Violation count

**Interpretation**:
- Bubbles in red zone (0.7-1.0) = **Critical risk**
- Large bubbles = High-volume suspicious activity
- Annotations on high-risk filings

### **4. Violation Breakdown**

**Purpose**: Statute distribution

**What It Shows**:
- Pie chart: Percentage of each violation type
- Bar chart: Absolute counts
- Full statute citations

**Interpretation**:
- Diverse violations = Systemic issues
- Single dominant violation = Focused fraud pattern
