WHAT CHANGED

HOW TO USE
================================================================================

EXAMPLE 1: First 10 Nike filings from 2019

  Date Range: Check

  Document Limit: Check, enter 10

EXAMPLE 2: Most recent 25 filings (any date)

  Date Range: Uncheck
  Document Limit: Check, enter 25
  Result: EXACTLY 25 most recent filings

EXAMPLE 3: All filings in Q1 2019
  Company: Nike
  Date Range: Check
    From: 2019-01-01
    To: 2019-03-31
  Document Limit: Uncheck
  Result: ALL filings in that quarter
